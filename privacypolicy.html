What information Qannas Service may collect about you?
We may collect the following types of personal information when you interact with Qannas Cargo Services: • Full name, address, phone number, and email address • Passport or visa details (for visa or travel services) • Delivery or shipping addresses and preferences • Payment or billing information • IP address, browser type, and device information when using our website • Preferences related to travel, cargo, or communication • Any other details you provide while contacting us, requesting a service, or participating in a promotion

In general, the personal information Qannas service collects includes (but is not limited to) name, address, contact details, gender, occupation.

How Qannas service will use information we collect about you?
Your information is used to: • Fulfill service requests (e.g., cargo shipping, visa processing, hotel bookings, tour reservations) • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries • Send important service updates or confirmations • Customize and improve our services and user experience • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements • Provide marketing communications (only if you have opted in)

Qannas Service only use your data for the purpose it was collected unless legally required or you give additional consent.

Qannas Servic may use Personal Information to communicate with you and to provide you with ongoing information about opportunities in which Qannas Service believes you may be interested.

In most cases where Qannas service asks you to provide Personal Information, if you do not provide Qannas Service with that information, Qannas Service will not be able to provide you with the requested product or service.
